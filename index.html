Traveller, runner, chemical engineer, skier, energy industry professional, and web developer. I attended the University of Florida where I earned a B.S. in Chemical Engineering. Following school, I started a career in the oil industry that took me to Baton Rouge, Louisiana for a little over 5 years before transferring to Billings, Montana. While working for one of the largest companies in the world, I was able to gain experience in project management and development, strategic planning, project economics, business operations, manufacturing, process optimization, and process monitoring. After a while, I started to feel a desire to push myself more, and to fulfill the desire to develop the creative side of my personality. I knew it was time to make a career change.

I took the opportunity between my careers to fulfill a few of dreams. Ski, travel, live overseas, and learn a new language. After finishing the winter of 2017 in Montanta, I travelled Europe for 2 months before moving to Berlin to learn German. Ich spreche ein bisschen Deutsch. While in Germany I began to explore my interest in web development, and decided that it was the perfect balance between creativity and my technical skills. I knew I had found my next career. At the moment, I am living in Orlando, FL while I attend the UCF Coding Bootcamp, and I am looking forward to starting a new career in a well balnced -technical and creative- field.
