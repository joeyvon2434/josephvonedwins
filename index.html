Traveller, runner, chemical engineer, skier, energy industry professional, and web developer. I attended the University of Florida where I earned a B.S. in Chemical Engineering. Following school, I started a career as a process engineer in manufacturing which took me to Baton Rouge, Louisiana for 5 years before transferring to Billings, Montana. While working for one of the largest companies in the world, I was able to gain experience in project management and development, strategic planning, project economics, business operations, manufacturing, and process monitoring and optimization. Eventually, I began to feel as if I wanted to be in a more creative field. I knew it was time to make a career change... I started saving.

I left my job and decided to fulfill a few of my dreams first; Ski, travel, live overseas, and learn a new language. After skiing for the winter of 2017 in Montanta, I travelled Europe for 2 months before moving to Berlin to learn German. Ich spreche ein bisschen Deutsch. While in Germany I began to explore my interest in web development, and decided it was the perfect balance between creativity and my technical skills. I knew I had found my next career. Currently, I am living in Orlando, FL and attendig the UCF Coding Bootcamp. I look forward to starting a new career in a well balnced -technical and creative- field.
